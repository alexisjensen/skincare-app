Friday AM

Routine

    Retinol adaptation phase

    This version is designed to help your skin get used to retinol. Exfoliation is paused for now.

    Morning — every day

    1. Cleanse with The Ordinary Glucoside Foaming Cleanser.
    2. Apply The Ordinary GF 15% Solution.
    3. Apply moisturizer: PDRN Hyaluronic Acid 100 Moisturizing Cream or The Ordinary Natural Moisturizing Factors + PhytoCeramides.
    4. Finish with SEPHORA COLLECTION Daily Sunscreen Cream SPF 30.
    5. Optional: apply Gopure Tighten & Lift Neck Cream to neck.
    Optional only if dry: use a small amount of The Ordinary 100% Fermented Rose Hip Seed Oil.

    Night rhythm

    Monday

    Retinol night

    • Cleanse
    • Retinol 1% in Squalane
    • Moisturizer

    Tuesday

    GF night

    • Cleanse
    • GF 15% Solution
    • Moisturizer

    Wednesday

    Peptides night

    • Cleanse
    • Multi-Peptide + Copper Peptides 1% Serum
    • Moisturizer

    Thursday

    GF night

    • Cleanse
    • GF 15% Solution
    • Moisturizer

    Friday

    Retinol night — optional

    • Cleanse
    • Retinol 1% in Squalane only if skin feels calm
    • Moisturizer

    Saturday

    GF night

    • Cleanse
    • GF 15% Solution
    • Moisturizer

    Sunday

    Barrier reset night

    • Cleanse
    • Soothing & Barrier Support Serum
    • Moisturizer

    Guardrails

    • Exfoliation is paused until your skin is comfortably tolerating retinol.
    • If your skin feels irritated, skip Friday retinol first.
    • If your skin feels overworked, replace peptides with the barrier serum.
    • Once your skin is comfortable with retinol twice a week for a few weeks, you can reintroduce exfoliation slowly.

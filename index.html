Weekday

Morning = before 5 PM. Night = 5 PM and later.

AM

Morning routine

  1. Cleanse with The Ordinary Glucoside Foaming Cleanser.
  2. Apply Vitamin C.
  3. Apply moisturizer: PDRN Hyaluronic Acid 100 Moisturizing Cream or The Ordinary Natural Moisturizing Factors + PhytoCeramides.
  4. Apply Gopure Tighten & Lift Neck Cream to neck.
  5. Finish with SEPHORA COLLECTION Daily Sunscreen Cream SPF 30.
Optional: if skin feels dry, add a very small amount of The Ordinary Squalane. Keep it light so sunscreen still sits well.
